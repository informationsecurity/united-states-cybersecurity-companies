# Firewall-as-a-Service (FWaaS) in United States in 2026: the best providers to protect enterprise networks in the cloud

In 2026, with the growing shift towards cloud environments, hybrid work, and distributed applications, **traditional perimeter security is no longer sufficient**. What's needed is flexible, scalable, and universally accessible protection: this is where **Firewall-as-a-Service (FWaaS)** comes in.

FWaaS enables the implementation of a centralized cloud-based firewall capable of protecting devices, offices, remote users, and cloud workloads without the need to install physical appliances. It's a fundamental pillar of **SASE (Secure Access Service Edge)** architectures and **Zero Trust** models.

In this guide, you'll find the **10 best United States FWaaS providers in 2026**, focusing on technology, scalability, integration, and compliance.
