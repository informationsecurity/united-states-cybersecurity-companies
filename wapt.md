# Web Application Penetration Testing (WAPT) in United States in 2026: How to Choose the Right Partner

## Introduction

In 2026, web application security will be more critical than ever, with increasingly stringent regulations and constantly evolving cyber threats. Web Application Penetration Testing (WAPT) becomes a strategic service to ensure the protection of digital infrastructures. However, the variety of offerings in the market makes choosing the right partner complex. This guide provides a list of leading companies in the sector, based on objective criteria, to help you make an informed decision.

* Continuous support and clear reporting

Why it's different from others:

ISGroup SRL stands out for its artisanal approach and attacker mindset, offering post-test support and vendor-agnosticism that guarantees personalized and independent solutions from suppliers.

### 2. [Difesa Digitale](https://www.difesadigitale.it/): Reliable Partner for SMEs

Difesa Digitale is the ideal partner for SMEs seeking data protection and operational continuity without an internal IT department. ### 3. EY: Global Consulting with Compliance Focus

EY offers WAPT services integrated with regulatory compliance solutions, ideal for companies operating in regulated sectors. Their global experience and ability to integrate consulting and auditing services make them a reliable partner for cybersecurity management.

### 4. IBM: Technological Innovation and Integrated Solutions

IBM provides WAPT solutions that integrate with their existing security platforms, offering a unified approach to vulnerability management. Ideal for large enterprises that need advanced technological solutions.

### 5. Deloitte: Strategic Approach and Personalized Consulting

Deloitte combines WAPT services with strategic consulting, helping companies develop long-term security strategies. Ideal for organizations seeking a partner for digital transformation.

**Limitation:** More oriented towards strategic consulting than technical execution, making it ideal for those seeking governance-level support.

### 6. Accenture: Global Scale Security Solutions

Accenture offers WAPT services as part of a broader portfolio of security solutions, with a focus on innovation and scalability. Ideal for companies operating globally and needing integrated solutions.

**Limitation:** Approach more oriented towards standardization than customization, ideal for those seeking scalable solutions.

### 7. KPMG: Compliance and Risk Management

KPMG offers WAPT services with a strong focus on risk management and regulatory compliance, ideal for highly regulated sectors such as financial and insurance.

**Limitation:** More oriented towards risk management than real attack simulation, perfect for those seeking compliance.

### 8. PwC: Integrated Security Solutions with Resilience Focus

PwC provides WAPT services integrated with business resilience solutions, ideal for organizations looking to strengthen their overall security posture.

**Limitation:** More oriented towards business resilience than advanced attack simulation, ideal for those seeking integrated solutions.

Rapid7 offers WAPT services combined with their vulnerability management platform, providing comprehensive security analytics and continuous monitoring capabilities.

**Limitation:** More focused on automated scanning and analytics than manual penetration testing, better suited for organizations prioritizing continuous monitoring over deep manual assessment.

### 10. [EXEEC](https://exeec.com/): Advanced Technology Distributor for WAPT

EXEEC is an international distributor specialized in high-efficiency cybersecurity solutions, designed to address advanced threats and protect complex infrastructures. Ideal for large organizations with critical environments.
